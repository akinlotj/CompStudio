FAQ
We provide in-app integration with ticket purchasing platforms such as EventBrite and Ticketmaster to buy your tickets and reserve your virtual concert at the same time.
With technology like adaptive bitrates and streaming servers provided via venue partnerships, VENu is able to bring the excitement of live concerts right into your home.
Once you’ve reserved your upcoming concert and purchased your tickets, your upcoming concerts will be available in the “Your Concerts” section.
If you’ve changed your mind about an upcoming concert, refunds can be provided through the ticket purchase platform you completed your transaction with.
VENu is currently a desktop platform, but will soon be available on mobile devices with added AR integration!
